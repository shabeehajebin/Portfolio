Skill

CIRCUIT DESIGN

Circuit design,it involves creating schematics and layouts for electronic circuits, ensuring efficient and reliable performance of electronic systems. It includes selecting appropriate components, designing the interconnections, and using CAD tools for precision and accuracy. Testing and simulation are crucial steps to validate the design before prototyping and production.

VLSI

VLSI (Very Large Scale Integration) involves designing integrated circuits (ICs) with millions of transistors on a single chip, enabling complex functionalities in compact sizes. It encompasses designing, layout, and fabrication processes using advanced semiconductor technologies. VLSI plays a pivotal role in modern electronics, powering devices from smartphones and computers to sophisticated medical equipment.

EMBEDDED

Embedded systems refer to specialized computing systems designed to perform dedicated functions within larger mechanical or electronic systems. They typically consist of a microcontroller or microprocessor, embedded software, and often include sensors and actuators.
